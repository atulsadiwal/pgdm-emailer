Scope of the PGDM Program

PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management) is a 2-year full-time program equivalent to an MBA. The curriculum covers Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, IT, Operations Management, Strategy, and Business Policy, providing a comprehensive education in all aspects of management. With an industry-aligned curriculum, this program prepares you for leadership roles, equipping you with the tools to excel in a dynamic global market.
